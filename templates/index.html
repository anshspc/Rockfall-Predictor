How the Predictor Works

Our predictive engine assesses slope stability by evaluating environmental and geological inputs. By processing core factors such as **slope angle, rock density, vibration levels, and precipitation**, the model classifies the hazard index to provide instantaneous warning alerts. This empowers engineers, planners, and public safety bodies to mitigate risks effectively.

Core Applications

Public Safety

Generates active risk assessment indicators to protect pedestrian trails, high-risk highways, and mountain settlements.

Infrastructure

Provides critical predictive metrics for railway lines, tunnel entrances, and high-altitude transport highways.

Geological Planning

Supports geologists and environmental planners with empirical models to implement preventive retaining structures.
