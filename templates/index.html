About the Model

Our Rockfall Prediction Model merges geological data, real-time environmental monitoring, and machine learning to estimate the likelihood of rockfall events. By evaluating slope angle, rock density, rainfall, temperature, and soil moisture, it delivers accurate, actionable risk assessments to keep people and assets safe.
